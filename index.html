Controls

Move mouse to aim (only while sighting down the cue), hold left-click to draw the cue back, release to shoot. Hold ALT + move mouse to set spin (X resets). V cycles the camera: down-the-stick aim → free fly-around (mouse looks, WASD move, Space/Shift up/down) → overhead. Click the table to lock the mouse; ESC releases it.

After a foul the other player gets ball in hand (overhead view — move the mouse to position the cue ball, then click to place it).
